## üí° Color Usage Tips

### **Primary Colors (60%)**
- Use for main backgrounds, large sections
- Creates the overall mood

### **Accent Colors (30%)**
- Use for buttons, highlights, CTAs
- Draws attention to important elements

### **Complementary Colors (10%)**
- Use sparingly for special elements
- Adds visual interest without overwhelming

### **Text Colors**
- Primary text: High contrast (white/light)
- Secondary text: Medium contrast
- Muted text: Low contrast for less important info
