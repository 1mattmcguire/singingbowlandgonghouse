Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about sound healing and our center.

What should I wear to a sound bath? +
We recommend loose, comfortable clothing that promotes relaxation. Layers are a good idea since you may cool down during the session.
Do I need any prior experience with meditation? +
No prior experience is needed. Our classes are open to all levels.
How long is a typical session? +
Sessions typically last between 45 and 60 minutes.
Is sound healing safe for everyone? +
Sound healing is safe for most people, but those with implants or sound sensitivity should consult their doctor.
