Our Mission

A Promise to Transform Lives

At Singing Bowl & Gong House, we believe that education is the foundation of a brighter future. Every year, we dedicate 1–2% of our profits to support underprivileged children from rural villages across Nepal. Our commitment goes beyond financial assistance — we provide school bags, stationery items, uniforms, and scholarships to ensure that no child is left behind.

We have proudly supported 1500+ students from remote rural areas, helping them access quality education and dream bigger. Our goal is simple yet powerful: to bring smiles through education and opportunity, one child at a time.
