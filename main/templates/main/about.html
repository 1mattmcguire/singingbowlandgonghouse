Our Story

Healing Through Vibrations

Since its inception, Singing Bowl and Gong House has known the incredible power of healing through sound and has celebrated its use as a tool for balancing and rejuvenation. Our vision is linked with balancing the mind, body, and spirit through our heavenly sound healing sessions. With more than 12 years of experience in teaching and practice at the national and international levels, authenticity and the highest quality have always been the essence of our work. Our tradition is rooted in the ancient knowledge that was transmitted to us by the revered Mangalman Maharjan, further enriched by more than 35 years of experience in sound healing. We feel very grateful to provide intensive therapies to our international clients from China, Hong Kong, Canada, the USA, and a pool of other nationalities. Sanctioned by the Government of Nepal, our services serve as a healing medium to lead the clients towards a state of relaxation, peace, and wellness.

Our Mission

What We Stand For

Holistic Healing & Personal Transformation

Our mission is to empower individuals to heal, grow, and reconnect with their inner wisdom through sound, mindfulness, and intentional community. We strive to create safe, inclusive spaces where transformation, emotional balance, and deep relaxation can unfold naturally.

Education & Skill-Building

We are dedicated to raising the standard of sound healing by offering immersive, ethical, and lineage-informed training. Our mission is to equip facilitators with the knowledge, creativity, and confidence to guide meaningful therapeutic experiences.

Community & Connection

We believe healing is amplified in community. Our mission is to cultivate circles of compassion, belonging, and shared intention—where individuals feel supported, seen, and inspired to explore their voice and gifts.

Mind-Body Wellness & Accessibility

Our mission is to make sound healing accessible and approachable for all backgrounds and experience levels. We aim to blend traditional wisdom and modern understanding to support nervous system regulation, emotional wellbeing, and spiritual grounding.
